Medi Ready

Pet Food Bowl

medi ready pet food bowl is a product that simplifies the process of giving medication to a variety of pets by crushing the pill and mixing the medication with pet food.

The lid and mixers fit onto any standard 6.5 inch bowl. This allows the product to be fully utilized for users with multiple pets.

The lid also functions to cover uneaten food which prevents bacteria build up, and projects your pet.
